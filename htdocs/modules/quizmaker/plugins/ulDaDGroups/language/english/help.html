ulDaDGroups: Group a list of terms by groups

This slide presents a list of items that must be placed in the correct groups.
There can be up to group.
The items move by a "Drag And Drop".
The items will be automatically mixed in the sjavascript slides.
To delete an item, empty the input area or check the "Delete" box
Options:

  • Orientation of the groups : they can be placed horizontally or vertically.

  • Width of items : The width of items can be modified depending on whether the labels of these items are longer or shorter.
                              This allows you to adjust the number of items per line if necessary and avoid overflow at the bottom of the slide.
  • Group label# : Only groups with a label will be taken into account.
                            The number of groups in the slide will therefore depend on the labels.
                            It will be displayed above and to the left of the group represented by a gray area.
  • Default group : Items can be grouped at launch in the same group or randomly.
